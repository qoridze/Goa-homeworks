Delicious Chocolate Cake

Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour the cake pans. Mix all ingredients together. Pour into pans and bake for 30 minutes.
